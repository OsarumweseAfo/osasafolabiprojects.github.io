About Me

As an experienced Data Analyst, I have a strong background in developing insightful reporting solutions using tools like Microsoft Excel, Power BI, Tableau, and SQL. I am eager to bring my expertise in data analysis and visualization to your esteemed organization. Passionate about leveraging data to drive growth and innovation, I focus on delivering impactful solutions aligned with business objectives. I specialize in managing and analyzing complex data sets to produce actionable insights that support product goals. My technical expertise ensures data structures and quality adhere to the highest standards. I thrive in collaborating with product management, data operations, and cross-functional teams to deliver results that meet stakeholder expectations. My proficiency in handling large databases, along with a detail-oriented, growth-driven mindset, enables me to extract valuable insights, even in fast-paced, international environments.

When not working on a project, I enjoy cooking and listening to music. These hobbies help me stay creative and focused, with music serving as a great way to relax and recharge, ultimately enhancing my productivity and performance.
